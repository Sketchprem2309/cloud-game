PROJECT OVERVIEW

Welcome to CLOUD GAMING: A Cloud Gaming Platform, a project dedicated to showcasing the potential of web-based gaming through JavaScript. This platform features a collection of interactive and engaging games, meticulously designed to provide a seamless gaming experience directly in your browser.

The goal of WebPlay is to combine entertainment with innovation, demonstrating how modern web technologies can be leveraged to create fun, dynamic, and user-friendly applications. From solving puzzles to slicing fruits, the platform caters to a wide range of interests and highlights the versatility of JavaScript in game development.
